Trade guide

Key

All letters except the last (normally)
D: Defective
E: Extremely
V: Very
U: Uncomfortable
S: Stone
A: Aluminium
Br: Brass
Q: Quite
St: Steel
Brz: Bronze
C: Comfortable
Cp: Copper
Sl: Silver
Pt: Platinum
G: Gold
M: Magic

Last letter (normally)
C: Chair
B: Bench
S: Shed
F: Factory
T: Throne
Tb: Thrench

3 Euc –> 1 Vuc
500 Euc –> 1 Aeuc
5 Vuc –> 1 Uc
3 Uc –> 1 Sc
5 Uc –> 1 Spuc
3 Sc –> 1 Ac
4 Sc –> 1 Brc
3 Spuc –> 1 Quc
2 Brc –> 1 Stc
5 Spuc –> 1 C
3 Stc –> 1 Brzc
3 C –> 1 B
2 C & 3 Quc –> 1 Cc
5 Stc -> 1 Cpc
3 B -> 1 S
3 S -> 1 F
75 Cpc -> 1 Slc
40 Slc -> 1 Ptc
2 Ptc -> 1 Gc
5 Gc -> 1 T
3 T -> 1 Tb
